About Color Palette Explorer

A colorful journey by Yota Kaizaki

The Colorful Backstory

Hey there! I'm Yota Kaizaki, and I have a confession: I'm slightly obsessed with colors. It all started when I accidentally spilled my entire collection of paint tubes during an art class in college. Instead of creating a mess, the colors mixed into the most beautiful combinations I'd ever seen.

That happy accident led me down a rabbit hole of color theory, design principles, and eventually coding. I created Color Palette Explorer because I couldn't find a tool that let me play with colors the way I wanted to â€“ something fun, intuitive, and visually stunning.

By day, I'm a digital nomad who designs interfaces for startups. By night, I'm constantly tweaking this tool, adding new features, and hunting for the perfect color combinations. My apartment walls are covered in color swatches, and my friends have banned me from commenting on their outfit color choices.

This project is my playground, and I'm thrilled to share it with fellow color enthusiasts. Whether you're a professional designer or just someone who appreciates beautiful colors, I hope this tool brings a little more vibrancy to your day!

Features & Tools

Single Colors

Explore individual colors with detailed information on HEX, RGB, CMYK values, plus tints, shades, and harmonies.

Color Palettes

Discover and create harmonious color combinations for your projects with our curated and user-generated palettes.

Visualizations

See your colors come to life with interactive visualizations including Fluid Waves, Glass Morphism, Gradient Blend, and Neon Glow.

Download your colors and palettes in various formats for use in your design projects.
